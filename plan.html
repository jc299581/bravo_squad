Mission Statement

Redesign the TCMC website to encourage more musicians to participate in events, more signups for membership and a higher sales of tickets.

Success Evaluation

Success will be evaluated by the amount of ticket sales and new memberships that are a result of the new website.

Target Audience

Music lovers between the ages of 20 and 30

  • List of volunteers needed for various organisations
